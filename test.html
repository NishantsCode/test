Key Themes

  • Mentoring: Discover new mentoring models that connect students with mentors, enhance guidance practices, and foster personal and professional growth. Learn how mentorship can bridge the skills gap and provide students with industry-aligned expertise.
  • Collaboration: Understand the power of collaboration between academia and industry, with a focus on creating impactful partnerships that bring relevant experiences to students. Engage with industry experts and institutions in building sustainable collaboration models.
  • Cultivation: Explore methods for nurturing talent through tailored training, resilience-building, and strategic feedback. Help students develop the competencies and mindset needed to thrive in the workforce.
